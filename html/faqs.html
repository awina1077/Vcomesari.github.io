Q and A

Q : What distinguishes VCO Mesari from normal coconut oil? A : A lot of the natural nutrients and flavor are removed during the refining, bleaching, and deodorizing processes used to make regular coconut oil. On the other hand, virgin coconut oil is not processed and keeps all of its taste and nutritional value. Q : How should I apply virgin coconut oil? A : Virgin coconut oil is very adaptable and useful in an extensive variety of applications. It functions well as a healthy cooking oil, a delightful and wholesome ingredient to smoothies and baked products, as well as a natural moisturizer for your skin and hair. Q : What is the shelf life of VCO Mesari? A :If properly maintained in a dry, cold environment, virgin coconut oil has a long shelf life and can last for up to two years. At temperatures below 76°F (24°C), it may become solid or hazy, but when warmed, it will become liquid again.
